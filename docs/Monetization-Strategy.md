## 2. Programmatic Ad Placement Strategy

While affiliate revenue provides high-value "hits," programmatic ads (via Google AdSense or Ezoic) provide a steady baseline of revenue based on traffic volume. I will implement the following ad units:

1.  **The "Sticky" Header Banner:** A 728x90 or 970x90 unit that remains visible as users interact with the tools.
2.  **In-Tool Native Ads:** Placed strategically between the "Input" and "Result" sections of each tool. This is high-engagement real estate.
3.  **Sidebar "Recommended Tools" Unit:** A 300x600 unit that can toggle between programmatic ads and your top-performing affiliate offers.
4.  **Exit-Intent Overlay:** A subtle, non-intrusive popup that appears when a user is about to leave, offering a "Top SEO Checklist" or a high-converting affiliate trial.

## 3. The "Revenue Stack" Implementation

I will build the platform using a **Configuration-First Architecture**. This means all monetization hooks are centralized in a single file (`shared/const.ts` or similar).

*   **Phase 1 (Build):** I will use "Smart Placeholders" that look like real ads/links but lead to a "Coming Soon" or a generic recommendation page.
*   **Phase 2 (Activation):** Once you provide your IDs, I will update the central config file, and the entire platform (all 15 tools) will be instantly monetized with your links.
