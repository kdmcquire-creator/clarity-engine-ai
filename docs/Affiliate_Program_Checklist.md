The programs are ordered by priority, focusing on those that are easiest to get accepted into and most relevant to a new site with low traffic.

## 5. Reapply Later (When Traffic Grows)

These programs are highly lucrative but typically require established traffic and a proven track record. It is best to wait until Clarity Engine has built a steady audience before applying.

**Semrush** offers one of the most comprehensive SEO tools available. Their affiliate program provides $200 per new subscription sale, $10 per new trial, and $0.01 per new sign-up, with a 120-day cookie duration [14]. However, acceptance is difficult for new sites, as they generally require a minimum of 1,000 monthly visitors or a strong social media following [15]. The action plan is to bookmark their [signup page](https://www.semrush.com/lp/affiliate-program/en/) and apply once Clarity Engine consistently hits 1,500+ monthly visitors.

**Ahrefs** is another major player in the SEO space, but they currently do not have a public affiliate program [16]. They discontinued it several years ago. The best approach is to monitor their announcements in case they reintroduce a program, but focus your promotional efforts on Semrush and other competitors for now.
